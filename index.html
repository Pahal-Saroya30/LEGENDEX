📖 LEGENDEX USER GUIDE

🎚️ 1. G.O.A.T. Algorithm

Drag the scoring sliders on the left (or select presets like Playmaker or Prime Peak) to weigh what defines greatness. The leaderboard updates instantly!

🔮 2. What-If Engine & Modal Profiles

Click any player card to inspect their stats. Click Edit Stats to modify values, and click Apply What-If to watch their rank change with live deltas (e.g. +2 ↑).

⚔️ 3. VS Arena (Comparison)

Compare up to 3 players side-by-side using the dropdowns. Inspect their overlay polygons on the dynamic Radar Chart and check head-to-head win predictions.

🛡️ 4. Build Dream XI Lineup

Click positions on the field pitch to assign legends. The system compiles the team OVR rating dynamically based on your current custom algorithm.

⚡ 5. Tournament Bracket

Seed the top 8 players into a knockout tournament. Run matchup simulations complete with synthesized whistles, stadium crown noise, and victory fanfares.

📊 6. Achievements & Spreadsheet

Complete 8 hidden tasks to unlock badges inside the Data Center. Click cells directly inside the interactive grid to modify records in real-time!
